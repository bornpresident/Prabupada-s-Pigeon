Hare Krishna

WhatsApp Message Sender

Popup closes when you pick a file. Use a tab to upload and see results.

1. Upload contacts (Excel or CSV)

Columns: Name, Phone Number, Gender (Male/Female). Include country code (e.g. 91). For Excel, save as CSV first.

2. Your message

First line is auto: "Hare Krishna <Name> Prabhu Ji," or "... Mata Ji," based on gender.

3. Delay between messages

Enter seconds (comma or space separated). For each message, a delay is picked randomly from your list. e.g. 3, 3, 5 or 2 4 6 8
